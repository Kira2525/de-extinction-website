Future Conservation Initiative

Future Conservation Initiative is a nonprofit organization focused on exploring scientific innovation responsibly. Our mission is to examine emerging technologies like de-extinction while making sure ecosystems, animal welfare, and ethical responsibility remain the top priorities.

While new genetic technologies may offer exciting possibilities, our organization believes that science should move forward with caution. We support research that aims to repair damage caused by humans, but only when it can be done without creating new risks for animals or the environment.

Our Goals

  • Promote ethical scientific research and responsible innovation
  • Educate the public on de-extinction using accurate, accessible information
  • Encourage transparency in scientific research
  • Support conservation efforts that protect endangered species

Future Conservation Initiative also works to raise awareness about the importance of protecting species that are currently at risk of extinction. We believe that conservation should always come first, and that de-extinction should never replace efforts to protect living ecosystems.

By combining education, research, and ethical discussion, our organization aims to help the public better understand both the potential benefits and serious concerns surrounding de-extinction.
